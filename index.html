    • Thirumanancheri Temple, Kumbakonam

    A popular Shiva temple located 26 km from Kumbakonam and 6 km from Kutthalam. It is also known as the Sri Kalyanasundareswarar Temple. The temple is associated with the marriage of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati, and devotees believe that a visit to the temple will remove obstacles to marriage. The Poosam star of the Chithirai month is considered auspicious at this temple.

    The legend The temple is associated with the marriage of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati. The story goes that Parvati wanted to marry only Shiva, and after meditating intensely, her prayers were answered. The place where she meditated became known as Thirumanancheri.

    The temple celebrates festivals like Navarathiri, Vijayadasami, Thirukkarthigai, and Aruda Dharshanam A unique ritual at the temple is for devotees to return with their spouses after marriage and wear the garlands they received when they prayed for an early marriage.
