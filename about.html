About

Thirumanancheri Temple is one of the most revered temples in Tamil Nadu. Located in the heart of Kumbakonam, it is dedicated to Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati. The temple is known for its deep association with marriage rituals, and it is believed that a visit here will remove obstacles to marriage. Thousands of devotees come every year to seek blessings.
