    A total of 6 Poojas are done on a daily basis in the Thirumanancheri Temple. Poojas such as Ushakkalam, Kalai Santhi, Uchikalam, Sayaratchai, Irandam Kalam, and Artha Jamam. Weekly poojas, including Sahasranamam and Lalitha Thiruchathi Archana, are also conducted on Tuesdays and Fridays.
